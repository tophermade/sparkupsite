SparkUp

Help & Support

SparkUp is an extremely simple and easy to use tool for creating better mood and spark boards.

If your question isn't covered here, don't hesitate to email support.

Getting Started

Creating your first mood board


After installing and starting SparkUp, create a new board by clicking the "New" button in the top right section. When you create your first board you will be prompted to select a save location for your mood board files. This is where all the images you add to your board will be kept. Most users make a new folder for this in their Home or Documents folders, but where have SparkUp store your files is up to you.

After selecting a save location, you'll create your first board by naming your board and then choose the square "add" button. And there is your shiny new mood board!

Adding Images

Adding via drag & drop


To add new local images to your board simply drag them to the white square icon on the bottom left, and release. Your image will be added and placed inside the viewport ready for placement.

Adding via url


To add new images via url to your board click the white square icon on the bottom left. A new window will slide in and you'll paste the url to the image (png, jpg, bmp, gif) into the field, and click the white square labeled "add". Your image will be added and placed inside the viewport ready for placement.

Manipulating Images

Move the board


To move the board simply click & drag the board anywhere the blue background is showing to reposition it.

Move an image


To move images around the screen simply click & drag the image you wish to reposition.

Resize an image


To resize an image click and drag the lower right corner of your image to your preference.

Delete an image


To delete an image simply double click, and a pop up will appear asking you to confirm or cancel the deletion.

That's really all there us to using SparkUp. Have fun! If you have an idea or feature request get in touch.
